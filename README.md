# Business-Case-Target-SQL
Analyzed 100K+ e-commerce orders using SQL to uncover customer behavior, sales trends, payment patterns, freight costs, and delivery performance. Generated actionable business insights through exploratory data analysis, KPI tracking, trend analysis, and state-wise performance evaluation to support data-driven decision-making.

🎯 Target Brazil E-Commerce Analytics | SQL Business Case Study

📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive SQL-based analysis of 100,000+ e-commerce orders from Target's Brazil operations between 2016 and 2018. The objective was to explore customer purchasing behavior, order trends, payment patterns, freight costs, and delivery performance to generate actionable business insights and strategic recommendations.

🚀 Business Problem

As a Data Analyst, the goal was to analyze Target Brazil's transactional data and answer critical business questions related to:

Customer distribution and market reach

E-commerce growth trends

Seasonal demand patterns

Revenue and order value analysis

Freight and logistics performance

Delivery efficiency

Payment behavior and installment usage

🛠️ Tools & Technologies

SQL (Google BigQuery),
Data Analysis,
Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA),
Business Intelligence,
Microsoft Excel,
Data Storytelling.


🔍 Key Analysis Performed
1. Exploratory Data Analysis:Analyzed dataset structure and data types,Identified order date range,Counted unique customer cities and states.
2. Order Trend Analysis:Year-over-Year order growth,Monthly seasonality analysis,Peak shopping period identification
3. Customer Behavior Analysis:Customer distribution across states,Purchase behavior by time of day:Dawn (0–6 hrs),Morning (7–12 hrs),Afternoon (13–18 hrs),Night (19–23 hrs).
4. Revenue & Economic Impact:Revenue growth analysis (2017 vs 2018),State-wise total and average order value,Freight cost analysis
5. Logistics & Delivery Performance:Delivery time calculations,Estimated vs Actual delivery comparison,Fastest and slowest delivery regions
Freight efficiency evaluation
6. Payment Analysis:Payment type trends,Installment usage analysis,Digital payment adoption patterns

📊 Business Insights

📈 Business Growth

Orders grew significantly between 2016 and 2018, reflecting rapid e-commerce expansion.

Strong year-over-year growth indicates increasing customer trust and platform adoption.

Seasonal demand peaks were observed during May, July, August, and year-end shopping periods.

👥 Customer Behavior

Customers were spread across 27 states and more than 4,000 cities, demonstrating extensive market coverage.

Afternoon and Night were the most active purchasing periods.

Customer concentration varied significantly by region, creating opportunities for localized marketing strategies.

💰 Revenue & Economic Impact

Total payment value increased substantially from 2017 to 2018.

Certain states contributed significantly more revenue than others.

E-commerce activity generated substantial economic movement through product sales and logistics operations.

🚚 Logistics & Delivery Performance

Many orders were delivered earlier than the estimated delivery date.

Freight costs varied significantly across states.

Some regions experienced longer delivery times, highlighting logistics optimization opportunities.

💳 Payment Trends

Credit Card was the dominant payment method.

Most customers preferred single-payment transactions over installments.

Alternative payment methods showed lower adoption rates.

💡 Recommendations

Customer Retention:Introduce loyalty programs and reward systems.

Implement customer segmentation using RFM Analysis.

Personalize offers based on purchase history.

Marketing & Sales:Increase promotional campaigns during seasonal peak periods.

Develop state-specific marketing strategies.

Focus acquisition efforts in underpenetrated regions.

Logistics Optimization:Establish regional fulfillment centers.

Improve last-mile delivery partnerships.

Optimize delivery routes to reduce freight costs.

Revenue Growth:Implement upselling and cross-selling initiatives.

Introduce bundled product offerings.

Increase average order value through targeted promotions.

Payment Strategy:Offer cashback and reward programs for digital payments.

Encourage single-payment transactions through incentives.

Expand payment flexibility while maintaining profitability.
